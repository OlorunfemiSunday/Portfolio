About Me
I am a young graphic designer and passionate about creating visually stunning designs that help brands grow. With more than 7 years of experience in the design industry, I think I could be a great asset to you as a Graphic Designer and web developer. I have experience as a freelance and in-house graphic designer, and I have worked on a range of print, interactive and digital design projects. Currently, I am the Graphic Designer at Bridgecard, where I design all communication materials and advertising campaigns. Our most recent campaign, which I worked with in conjunction with the marketing team, increased our sales by 20% since launch.

Currently running my frontend development program with Vephla Institute, I have a BA in English Language and expertise with CorelDraw, Photoshop, After Effects and Microsoft Office.
